Sesame Street® was created by Joan Ganz Cooney and Lloyd Morrisett in the United States. It is an educational television show that teaches children the value of education and friendship across the world. Robotics Club of Vanier College is simply using Sesame Street® as their theme for the kiosk, the website, and the video for the Flip2020 competition of CRC. We do not own any of the intellectual properties of Sesame Street®. We chose Sesame Street® as our theme because we wanted to invoke the nostalgia of our childhood and to teach other kids the joys of robotics.
